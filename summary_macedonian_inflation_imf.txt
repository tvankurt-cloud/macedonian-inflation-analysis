 North Macedonia Economic Analysis Summary
 Based on IMF GVAR Model Study 2025

 Key Findings

1. **External Shock Impact**
   - Western Balkan economies faced multiple external shocks
   - Increasing integration with European Union observed
   - North Macedonia shows highest sensitivity to spillovers

2. **Economic Integration Effects**
   - Significant impact from Euro area real shocks
   - Strong influence on economic activity and trade
   - Deep integration into European global value chains

3. **Monetary and Financial Impacts**
   - Financial shock effects moderated by:
     * Financial depth
     * Integration levels
   - Both conventional and unconventional monetary policies affect:
     * Economic activity
     * Inflation rates

4. **Inflation Drivers**
   - Global energy commodity prices identified as major factors
   - Monetary policy effectiveness varies with autonomy level
